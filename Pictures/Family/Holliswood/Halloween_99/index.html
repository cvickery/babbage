Halloween 1999

Here are a couple of shots of Alex dressed in the tunic and ruffled shirt that Nancy made for him. What you can't see is the pony tail that goes perfectly with the outfit. He wore it to school with his hair sprayed black on Friday, and people didn't recognize him.

The costume was definitely a joint effort between Nancy and Alex. They went to Fabric Bonanza together to pick out the pattern and material without knowing ahead of time exactly what they would be doing. The shirt is one that Josh used when he was Robin Hood, with the frills added for this outfit.

Oh yes, you can't see the dracula teeth either ...
