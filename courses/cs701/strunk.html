There are many handbooks that give principles of good English language writing style. One of the best known is Elements of Style by William Strunk Jr. and E. B. White, which was published by the Macmillan Company in 1959. That book is a modified version of a student handbook written by Professor Strunk at Cornell University in 1918.

There is an on-line copy of the 1918 version of Elements of Style by William Strunk Jr. at http://www.cc.columbia.edu/acis/bartleby/strunk/
