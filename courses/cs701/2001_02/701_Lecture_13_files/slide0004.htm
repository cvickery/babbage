Using makedepend
• makedepend interprets all preprocessor directives to
determine what header files are referenced by .c or .cc
files, and generates dependency lines for your Makfile
– Follows nested #includes, which isn’t always what you want.
 makedepend -Y *.cc
– Makefile must be writeable; header file dependencies will be put at
the and of it after a line that says, “# DO NOT DELETE …”
• The depend target:
 depend:
 <tab>makedepend –Y $(SRCS)
– “What’s $(SRCS)?” I hear you ask …
