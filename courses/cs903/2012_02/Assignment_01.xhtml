Look at a few interviews on The Setup to get an idea of the types of answers prople give there. Then write your own answers to the questions:
  • Who are you, and what do you do?

    We know you are student at Queens College, but give some more details. What year are you in? What majors or minors are you taking, or planning to take? What do you do outside of school that might relate to this course?

  • What hardware are you using?

    Computers, phones, ...

  • And what software?

    Applications, operating systems, ...

  • What would be your dream setup?

A Word document is probably the simplest form, but I’ll accept anything electronic, so long as it’s easy to read.

Brief is good.
