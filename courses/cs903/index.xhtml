Computer Science 903 (Formerly CSCI 90.3)

Courses

CSCI-903 is the Computer Science Department’s “Special Topics” course number that we use for new courses in the CIT Minor. Under CUNYFirst, the course number has been transmogrified from 090.3 into 903. But that doesn’t mean it’s become a graduate-level course!

I have taught two different courses under the 90.3 rubric:

  • Script Programming for the Web.
  • Information and Intelligence. This course is now CSCI 100.
