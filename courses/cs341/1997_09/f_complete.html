NAND, NOR, and MPX are Functionally Complete

The assignment was to prove that two-input NAND, NOR, and MPX are "functionally complete" by implementing AND, OR, and NOT using only the gates specified. (Actually, a multiplexor isn't a gate, but the idea is the same.) They are called functionally complete because we know we can implement any logic function using AND, OR, and NOT to build a sum-of-products solution.
