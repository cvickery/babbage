Two’s Complement Subtraction
• Negate the minuend (second operand), and add.
– X-Y = X+(-Y)
• ALU design: just one circuit for adding, plus a
small amount of logic for negating the minuend
instead of a whole other circuit for subtracting.
– Simpler to design and faster execution.
• A carry out of the leftmost position is normal, and
may be ignored.
– May be used for multiple-precision arithmetic.
