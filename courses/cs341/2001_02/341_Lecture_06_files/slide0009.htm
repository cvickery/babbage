Two’s Complement Overflow
• If the result of an addition is bigger than +2n-1-1 or
less than -2n-1, the correct answer cannot be
represented with n bits, and overflow has occurred.
• Carry into the leftmost position will be unequal to
the carry out of the leftmost position iff overflow
occurs.
– Adding a positive value to a negative value cannot
overflow.
– Add two positives: sign bit of 1 indicates overflow
– Add two negatives: sign bit of 0 indicates overflow
