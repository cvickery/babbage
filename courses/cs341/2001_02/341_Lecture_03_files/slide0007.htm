Encoding Text: ISO Latin-1
• International Standards Organization (ISO)
• 8-bits per character
• First 128 characters are the same as ASCII 0x00
through 0x7F.
• Can represent European languages with diacritical
marks (à á â ã ä å, etc.)
• Also known as ISO-8859-1
• Other ISO Latin-n codes for other sets of
alphabets.
