PROM Properties
I inputs; O outputs
• Inputs are addresses; Outputs are data
• There are always 2I AND gates
– No fuses between inputs and AND gates
– The fan-in of each AND gate is I
• There are 2I * O fuses between the AND gates and the OR
gates
• Each AND gate must have a fan-out of O or greater
• Each OR gate has a fan-in of 2I
• Can be used as a memory, or to implement up to O
functions of I variables.  No limit on the number of
minterms
