Encoding Audio Information
• A transducer converts sound pressure to voltage.
– Microphone.
• Voltage is periodically sampled and converted to
binary numbers by an Analog to Digital Converter
(ADC) circuit.
– Nyquist Theorem: Sampling rate must be two times the
highest frequency to be reproduced.
• Human auditory sensitivity ends at about 20KHz.
• CD standard is 44KHz per channel.
– Number of bits per sample determines how many
different pressure levels can be reconstructed.
• CD standard is 12 bits per sample.
