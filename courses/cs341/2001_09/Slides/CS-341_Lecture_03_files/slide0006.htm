Two’s Complement
• Virtually always used for fixed-point arithmetic
– Integer is special case of fixed-point
• One zero
• Monotonic, with discontinuity between zero and largest negative value
• Range for n bits is –2n-1 to +2n-1-1
• Negate by complementing and adding one
– Works for negative, positive, and zero, but not smallest negative value
• Sign bit has a negative weight
– Sign Extension
• Ignore leftmost carry when adding
• Subtract by adding the negation of the subtrahend (bottom number)
from the minuend (top number)
• Overflow detected when carry into sign bit differs from the carry out
