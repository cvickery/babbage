Assignment:

This assignment is due May 1, 1998.

Draw the gates to implement a clocked D latch and to implement a clocked D flip-flop (master-slave).

Construct timing diagrams for both the latch and the flip-flop to illustrate their behavior. You are to make up a "interesting" sequences of inputs and show the resulting outputs. Be sure to illustrate how the two differ in their timing with respect to the clock, show how to turn them on and off, and anything else you think would be interesting.
