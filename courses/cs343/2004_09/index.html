Assignments

Initial Background Assignments:

  • Read chapters 1 and 2 of the textbook on your own. In addition to the background material in chapter 1, study chapter 2 well enough to understand the MIPS assembly and machine language. But you do not need to read sections 2.7 through 2.20 for this course.
  • Study all of Appendix B.
  • If you are not familiar with CircuitMaker yet, read my Introduction to CircuitMaker web page, download the software, and practice using it to construct and simulate a simple combinational logic circuit, such as a single full adder built from AND, OR, and Invert gates.
  • Optional, but useful: Read and do the exercises in what's currently available of "Chapter 0" from a text that I'm working on. Any feedback you supply to me would be appreciated. And it should provide you with a good review of material you should already be familiar with.
