Logic Kit Pin Assignments

The following links take you to pin assignment tables for the three Altera logic design kits available for use in this course. All kits have additional devices connected to their FPGAs; only clocks, switches, buttons, LEDs, and seven segment displays are listed. Some of the clocks are intended for specific functions, such as video output. When in doubt about a clock, consult your instructor or the manual for the the kit.

UP2

Eight DIP switches, Two pushbuttons, and two seven segment displays.

UP3

Four DIP switches, Four pushbuttons, and four LEDs.

DE1

Ten slide switches, four pushbuttons, four seven segment displays, ten red LEDs, and eight green LEDs.
