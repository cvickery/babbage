Homework Assignments

Homework assignments are provided to help you practice the material covered in the course. The homework assignments provide a model for the types of questions that will be asked on exams.

Homework assignments are not graded, but I will record each one you submit, regardless of how well you answer the questions. A small percentage of your course grade (1-2%) will be based on what proportion of the homework assignments you submit on time. You are free to work with others on the assignments, and to use the Discussion Board at the Queens College Blackboard site for the course to work on the homeworks with others.
