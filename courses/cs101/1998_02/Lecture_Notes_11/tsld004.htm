Applets and Applications

  • Application: JVM (java.exe) loads class and calls main()

  • Applet: Applet Context (appletviewer or browser) loads class, instantiates it, and calls init(), start(), stop(), paint().

    • Security restrictions: No disk I/O, among others. Browsers may accept signed applets.
    • Implications for WordFind 2
