Container.validate()
Causes the container’s layout manager to re-calculate the container’s geometry.
Trickles down to subcontainers, so brute force way get a checkbox to show up on the screen is to call validate() on the JFrame that holds the content pane that holds the scrollpane that holds the panel that holds the checkbox.
