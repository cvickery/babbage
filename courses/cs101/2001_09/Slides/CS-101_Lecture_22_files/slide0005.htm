To Build a GUI
• Establish the layout(s) to use.
– Border, Flow, Grid, …
• Create Container(s) and add Component(s) to
them.
– Swing’s JFrame has a “Content Pane” that acts as the
container.  BorderLayout is default.
• Establish listeners to respond to events generated
by the components.
• Compute the actual geometry of the GUI
– pack()
• Display the GUI on the screen
– show() or setVisible() on the top-level window.
