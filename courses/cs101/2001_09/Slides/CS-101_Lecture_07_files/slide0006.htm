The final Modifier
• Once a final item has been defined, it cannot be
changed.
• Enforces the principle of least privilege.
– If it doesn’t make sense to change something, make it
so the compiler will consider any attempt to do so an
error.
• May be applied to fields and/or methods.
– Makes fields into constants.
•  public final double Π = 3.14159;
– Makes methods non-overloadable.
