MusicMatch Jukebox
• Download free copy:
–  http://www.musicmatch.com
• Can play MP3 music files.
• Can generate MP3 music files from audio CDs.
• Maintains a “library” of your MP3 music files.
– Provides an editor for your library.
• There are other MP3 programs available, such as
winamp, realaudio jukebox, etc.
