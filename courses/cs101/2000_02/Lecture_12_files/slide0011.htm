Subclasses and Superclasses
• A subclass inherits methods and fields from its
superclass, super-superclass, etc.
– A subclass object therefore is a superclass object, but
with additional features.
– A superclass may override some or all of its superclass’
methods.
• Exercise: Write a superclass and a subclass that
both implement a method named
identifyYourself(), but differently from each other.
– Have the subclass provide an additional method,
saySomethingNice().
