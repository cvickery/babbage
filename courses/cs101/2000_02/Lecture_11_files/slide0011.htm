Threads
(“Enrichment Topic”)
• Most common case: One thread of execution,
which “starts” in main() and executes statements
sequentially. (Why is “starts” in quotes?)
– Loops and Method calls alter the sequence of statement
execution.
• Multiple threads can be created; they run
concurrently.
– But on a single-CPU computer, they actually take turns.
– One use is for animating applets.
