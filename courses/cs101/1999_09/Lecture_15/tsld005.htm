Classes for Character I/O

  • FileReader, FileWriter

    • Simple character I/O, but relatively slow.
    • Key methods: read(), write(), and close().
    • Unicode characters get converted to ASCII by default.

  • BufferedReader, BufferedWriter

    • Faster than FileReader and FileWriter.
    • Can handle text lines as Strings.
      • BufferedWriter.newLine()
      • BufferedReader.readLine()
