Project 3 Frequently Asked Questions

Question: Why doesn't my board show the change when I call cb.init() and cb.zero() in the actionPerformed() methods for my buttons?

Answer: I gave some bad advice in class, suggesting that you could just call the validate() method for the Panel that holds all the squares. That does work for my program, but I'm not sure why! Calls to a container's validate() method are supposed to be ignored if the container or its contents haven't changed size. Instead, you should loop through all the black square Panels, and call their repaint() method to get them to show whatever piece they are supposed to have on them. There are optimizations you could do to reduce the number of method calls you have to make, but this technique works and I don't think you will see any effect of the extra overhead. You will need to use this technique when you start moving pieces, too.
