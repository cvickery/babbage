Positional Number Systems

Write an explanation of positional number systems, as described in class on September 4.

Write your explanation on paper, to hand in during class on the due date.

Write your explanation for an audience of students who are already familiar with decimal numbers and exponents. Include examples of at least three positional number systems (such as binary, octal, decimal, or hexadecimal) to help make your explanation clear. Limit you explanation to one page or less.

The assignment may be hand-written if your handwriting is clear. Spelling, grammar, accuracy, and clarity all count.
