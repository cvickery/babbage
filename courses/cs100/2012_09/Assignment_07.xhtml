CSCI 100 Assignment 7

Human Human Chapters 0-3

Video Assignment

How to sketch with electronics.

Reading Assignment

Read chapters 0-3 of the Christian book.

Questions

Write a well-structured paragraph to answer each of the following questions.

  1. Describe what you would build if you had access to one of the kits shown in the video. Tell what it would do and how it would work.
  2. Claude Shannon married a computer. Explain.
  3. Ivana was a robot. Explain.
  4. Make gramatically correct version of “the sentence” using the idea of a “featherless biped” in the second part. Does this seem like a good definition?
  5. “... the Turing test is, at the bottom, about the act of communication." (p13) How did Shannon's development of information theory also deal with “the act of communication?”
  6. Describe the Turing test and what it attempts to determine.
  7. Why is the Turing test like speed dating?
  8. Google uses Artificial Intelligence to figure out the best answers for queries, in part, by keeping track of which links people click on when given search results. They use a similar technique for doing translating among languages, see How does it work? Explain how this relates to the description of how Cleverbot works.
  9. Does Brian Christian think the Google approach would be good for translating a novel?
  10. The section on “Statefulness” explains the concept of a state-less conversation. State is also an essential component in the design of the Teagueduino binary counter. Explain how the concepts of “state” and “next” apply to the binary counter.
  11. What claim did Desartes make about the pineal gland?
  12. If you were a judge in a Turing test contest, how would you find out if there is a soul at the other end of the line?
